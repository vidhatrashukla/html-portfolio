About Me

Hello! I'm Vidhatra Shukla, a 22-year-old product management professional passionate about building technology that creates meaningful impact at scale.

My Journey

I graduated with a BE in Chemical Engineering and a minor in Finance from BITS Pilani, where I discovered my love for solving complex problems through the intersection of technology and business strategy. What started as an engineering education evolved into a passion for product management, where I can combine analytical thinking with user empathy to build solutions that matter.

Currently, I work as an Associate Product Manager at FarMart, a Series-C funded agritech company pioneering sustainable rural development. Here, I focus on improving platform scalability, implementing automation solutions, and helping scale revenue through resilient technology architecture.

Professional Experience

My career spans diverse sectors—from renewable energy at Suzlon Group to logistics at GoSwift—each teaching me valuable lessons about adaptability and data-driven problem solving. Whether it's improving delivery success rates by 6% through call-routing optimization or automating emission accounting to reduce manual effort by 65%, I thrive on turning ambiguous challenges into measurable outcomes.

During my time at university, I led the placement committee through a challenging hiring downturn, achieving a 77% placement rate above the national average and pioneering partnerships with venture capital firms. These leadership experiences taught me the importance of building consensus across diverse stakeholders and driving innovation under pressure.

What Drives Me

I'm fascinated by the potential of technology to democratize access and create inclusive solutions. From developing safety tracking apps for industrial environments to working on financial inclusion through digital payments, I believe in building products that serve everyone, not just the privileged few.

My analytical mindset, shaped by my finance background, helps me approach product decisions with both user empathy and business acumen. I enjoy diving deep into market analysis, whether it's researching Indian stocks for investment decisions or analyzing business metrics for startups.
